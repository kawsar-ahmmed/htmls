🧠 SaaS Growth Architecture — Internal Brief
Why our 15 events split into two completely different jobs
Most people see "15 Meta events" and think they all do the same thing. They don't. 3 of them talk directly to Meta's AI optimizer. The other 12 build the audience buckets that make retargeting intelligent. Understanding the difference is what separates a team that scales from one that burns budget.
The two-layer architecture
🤖
Layer 1 — Optimizer
Standard Events (3)
Train Meta's AI to find buyers
  • Meta's algorithm reads these to build a behavioral profile of "who converts"
  • Powers your campaign objectives — Leads, Conversions, ROAS
  • The optimizer gets measurably smarter every ~50 conversions it sees
  • Without these, Meta is showing ads to random people
  • Events: CompleteRegistration · Subscribe · Purchase
🪣
Layer 2 — Audiences
Custom Events (12)
Sort users into retargeting buckets
  • Meta's optimizer cannot use these — they don't train the algorithm
  • Used in Audiences Manager to build precise Custom Audiences
  • Each event = a bucket of users at a specific drop-off point
  • Without these, your retargeting is "everyone who visited the site"
  • 9 behavioral events → 9 distinct retargeting segments
